Same Pit, Same Family, Six Decades

Jack's opened its doors on Route 66 in Oklahoma City back in 1963, and the pit out back has been burning post oak ever since. Three generations of pitmasters, one method, no shortcuts.

Travelers rolling through on the Mother Road stop for the same brisket regulars have been ordering since Kennedy was in office. That is not nostalgia talking, that is just how long it takes to get barbecue right.
